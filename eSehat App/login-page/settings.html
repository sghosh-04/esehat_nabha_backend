FAQs

How do I book an appointment?

To book an appointment, go to the Appointments section and click on "Book New Appointment". Select your preferred doctor, date, and time slot.

How can I access my medical records?

Your medical records are available in the Medical Records section. You can view, download, or share them with healthcare providers.

What should I do if I forgot my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. You'll receive instructions to reset your password via email or SMS.
