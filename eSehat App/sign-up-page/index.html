Available Voice Commands:

Form Navigation:

  • "Switch to patient sign up" - Switch to patient form
  • "Switch to doctor sign up" - Switch to doctor form
  • "Next field" - Move to next input field
  • "Previous field" - Move to previous input field

Patient Form Commands:

  • "Enter full name [name]" - Enter full name
  • "Enter email [email]" - Enter email address
  • "Enter mobile number [number]" - Enter phone number
  • "Enter date of birth [date]" - Enter birth date
  • "Select gender [male/female/other]" - Select gender
  • "Enter password [password]" - Enter password
  • "Enter address [address]" - Enter address
  • "Enter city [city]" - Enter city
  • "Enter pincode [code]" - Enter pincode

Doctor Form Commands:

  • "Enter doctor name [name]" - Enter doctor name
  • "Enter doctor email [email]" - Enter professional email
  • "Enter doctor phone [number]" - Enter doctor phone
  • "Select specialization [specialization]" - Choose specialization
  • "Enter qualifications [qualifications]" - Enter qualifications
  • "Enter license number [number]" - Enter license number
  • "Enter clinic name [name]" - Enter clinic name
  • "Enter clinic address [address]" - Enter clinic address
  • "Enter experience [years]" - Enter years of experience

General Commands:

  • "Submit form" - Submit the current form
  • "Clear form" - Reset the current form
  • "Agree to terms" - Check terms and conditions
  • "Show password" / "Hide password" - Toggle password visibility
  • "Help" - Show this help dialog
