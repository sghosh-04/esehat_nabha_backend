Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment online?

You can book an appointment by logging into your account, selecting the "Appointments" section, choosing your preferred doctor, and selecting an available time slot. You'll receive a confirmation email once your appointment is scheduled.

What are your operating hours?

Our facility is open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and on weekends from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Emergency services are available 24/7.

How can I access my health records?

You can access your health records by logging into your account and navigating to the "Health Records" section. All your medical history, prescriptions, and test results will be available there.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we have a 24/7 emergency department. For critical emergencies, please call our emergency hotline at +91 9876543210 or visit our facility immediately.
